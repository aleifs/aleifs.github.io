I am an Assistant Professor at the [São Paulo School of Business Administration (FGV-EAESP)](https://eaesp.fgv.br/) and a member of the Rede de Economistas Pretas e Pretos. I hold a PhD in Economics from the [São Paulo School of Economics (FGV-EESP)](https://economics-sp.fgv.br/), an MSc in Economics and a bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the [Federal University of Bahia (UFBA)](https://www.ufba.br/). My research is in the field of development economics, specifically addressing topics related to education, labor markets, health, and productive inclusion.
